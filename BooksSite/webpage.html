Welcome to Brian's Reads!



Here I will be discussing the books and authors of books series that I have read and have found to be good or worth a read. Most of (all) these books are of the fantasy fiction genre. Who doesn't like a little fantasy fiction?? Am I right?

Cirque Du Freak


Let's start this off with the first book series I've ever read. You can also say this is the series that also got me hooked on fantasy fiction. I can honestly say that this series is a must read.

Author: Darren Shan
Brian's rating 9/10

Alex Rider


This was a highly enjoyable series about spys and if I remember correctly was my second book series read after Cirque Du Freak. If you decide to pick up this book, just know that it won't let you down.

Author: Anthony Horowitz
Brian's rating 7.5/10

Percy Jackson


No, not the movies. I'm talking about the original book series written by Rick Riordan. This series is all about Percy and his adventures when his world clashes with the world of the Olympians. Look, I'm not going to lie, this series isn't going to be eveyone's cup of tea. But if you are ready for a light-hearted, fun and exciting read then try it out!

No!! I'm not talking about the movies!

Author: Anthony Horowitz
Brian's rating 7/10

Ender's Game


Why do so many good books have to be re-made into movies? Anyways, the movie reiteration wasn't half bad and guess what? The book series is even better (big surprise there). Enders game is a futuristic, military vs intergalactic space aliens, themed book and I highly recommend this.

Author: Orson Scott Card
Brian's rating 8/10

Steelhart - The Reckoners


What would happen to the world if the criminals/villans had superpowers? Another person with powers would save the day right? Well now imagine a world where only the bad guys had powers and the good guys had... well.. guns?

Step into this world with The Reckoners as they try to fend off superpowered criminals. Will they prevail? Try this series out to find out!

Author: Brandon Sanderson
Brian's rating 7.5/10

Mistborn


Powers like you have never seen before. Brandon Sanderson puts on his thinking hat as this is one of the most imaginitive series that I have ever read. This triology was a great and it can take you along for a dizzying ride as the world building is stunning and, as you would put it, out of this world!

Also, don't forget about the tree book sequel as I believe it is a MUST READ after finishing the main series.

Author: Anthony Horowitz
Brian's rating 8.5/10
